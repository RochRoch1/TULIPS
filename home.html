Types of Tulips Flower

Single Early Tulip

Short strong stemmed varieties making them ideal for containers and garden borders in windy areas. These lovely cup-shaped tulips come in a wide range of colours.

Single Late Tulip

Also known as Cottage Tulips, are renowned for their large, cup-shaped flowers that exude classic elegance.

Double Early Tulip

Short stems with fully double flowers which can be up to 8 cm across giving a peony-like appearance. The flowers are long-lasting and well-suited for cut flowers.

Double Late Tulip

Also known as Peony Tulips, bring a burst of opulence of the garden with their lush , multi-layered blooms and vibrant colors.

Lily-Flowering Tulip

Very distinctive and elegant, with graceful stems bearing single, goblet-shaped flowers with pointed, reflexed petals. These late bloomers are long lasting and excellent for bouquets.

Viridiflora Tulip

With their distinctive green streaks running through each petal, bring an element of sophistication to the spring garden.

Kaufmanniana Tulip

Also known as Waterlily Tulips, bring a splash of early spring color to your garden with their unique star-shaped blooms that resemble waterlilies.

Greigii Tulip

Whether planted in garden beds, rock gardens, or containers, Greigii Tulips splash of color and a touch of elegance to any spring garden.
